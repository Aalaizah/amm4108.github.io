Smoke Test

Published: Wed 25 February 2015
By Alexandria Mack

In Development.

tags: HFOSS

While in the middle of my first smoke test, the first OLPC decided that I didn't need to be able to access anything other than the left third of the screen. Performing the smoke test on the second machine I used went perfectly fine.

It's an interesting little piece of machinery. I'm interested in seeing how well the game we end up making will work on it, and what sort of tweaks we may have to make in order to make it run better.
